ABOUT ME

Hello, This is Huq

I am from Dhaka and I recently completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at BRAC University. During my time in university, I explored various aspects of computer science, including machine learning, artificial intelligence and web development. Among these, I discovered a true passion for web design and Software Quality Assurance, which I'm excited to pursue as I embark on my career.
While studying, I also had the opportunity to participate in research projects, and I'm proud to say that I co-authored several research papers. Two of these papers were even published in 2023 & 2024. I'm currently working on another exciting research project. My main area of interest in research is Human-Computer Interface, a field I'm hoping to delve deeper into during my postgraduate studies.
Beyond my academic interests, I have a few hobbies that keep me busy. I enjoy taking mobile photography and experimenting with cartoon drawing, allowing me to express my creativity in different ways. When I'm not busy with my studies or hobbies, you can often find me engaged in online gaming, watching TV shows and anime, or enjoying the great outdoors. I'm particularly fond of cycling, which is not only a great way to stay active but also a fantastic way to clear my mind and enjoy nature. My varied interests reflect my love for learning and experiencing new things, and I'm excited to see where my journey in the world of web design and Human-Computer Interface research takes me.
